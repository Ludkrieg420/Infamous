Guild rules

Core rules to follow

1. Respect the roster.
No harassment, hate speech, targeted toxicity, or unnecessary drama.
2. Stay prepared.
Know your class, bring the right setup, and enter runs ready to perform.
3. Communicate early.
If you miss a key or raid, give notice instead of wasting other people’s time.
4. Accept accountability.
Mistakes happen. Own them, learn from them, and move on without excuses.
Standards

What members are expected to do

5. Protect guild resources.
Use guild bank materials, consumables, and repairs responsibly.
6. Follow event leadership.
During keys and raids, callouts from leaders take priority over ego plays.
7. Stay active.
We are building around players who are consistently present in game and on Discord.
8. Earn your rank.
Promotions are based on trust, activity, and performance rather than time alone.
